//*$$frmManChordSync.$chkNoSkipAfterReloc //*

If checked, avoid moving the next chord forward after relocating the current position.

//*

This could be after playing a single note action key, or setting a new location (e.g. clicking to a new position).

//*

This will also move the position forward by 2 chords when a single action key is played.

//*

This may make the playing easier, but is really a matter of personal preference. It's easier to try this than to actually explain it!
