How To Play a MidiFile

If you haven't already done so, load a Project containing a MidiFile and a ChordFile

Open A Playable Window

You can play a MidiFile from either the PlayMap window, the TrackMap window, or the ChordMap window.

All of these windows have buttons to start, stop, and pause play, and they all keep track of the current play position.

The TrackMap

This shows the notes of each track of the MidiFile. You can mute and solo tracks from here, adjust the volume and panning of any track, and change the patch (instrument) of any track.

Any changes made here can be saved in the ChordFile (.chp).

The PlayMap

This is where you would normally play along to the MidiFile using your midi or PC keyboard. It shows the current position in the MidiFile in a display that shows the generated chords rolling downwards as the music progresses.

This makes it easier to improvise and play along with the MidiFile - see HowTo Play a melody along with a MidiFile and HowTo play chords along with a MidiFile.
