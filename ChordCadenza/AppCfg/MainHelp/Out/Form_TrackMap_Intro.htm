The TrackMap

The TrackMap is the main window for managing and playing midi tracks.

It shows the notes for each track of the MidiFile.

It allows you to select tracks for generating chords, mute and solo tracks, and change the volume, panning, and patch (instrument) for each track.

You can also display the lyrics with this.

By default, all tracks from the MidiFile are shown, but you can click the Track Styles Button to bring up a window to manage which tracks are visible.
