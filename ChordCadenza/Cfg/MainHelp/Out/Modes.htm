Modes

There are 2 Modes used by this program:

KeyBoardMode

All of the keys of the midi keyboard are set to their normal pitches (subject to any transposition that may be active).

This may be used with the KBChord SwitchKey, which will play the nearest chord note to the key actually played.

ChordMode

Only the white keys of the midi keyboard are active, and these are assigned to chord notes.
